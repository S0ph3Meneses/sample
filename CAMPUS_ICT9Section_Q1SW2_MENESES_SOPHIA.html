CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Ibarra Crisóstomo
The protaginist and only offspring of his father Don Rafael Ibarra. He is arranged to be married to Maria Clara and friend of Elias. Father Dámaso probably hates his guts.

Maria Clara
Ibarra's beautiful lover and apparently illegitimate child of Father Dámaso.

Capitan Tiago
Maria Clara's father and influential buisness man.

Father Dámaso
Arrogant and selfish Spanish priest. Maria Clara's biological father.

Doña Victorina
Victorina de los Reyes de De Espadaña, a woman who passes herself off as a Peninsular.
